About

Maodong Studio was established in 2020. Our company is an animation company with rich experience in producing drama animations, CG movies, and game CG. Our company adheres to the mission of producing high-quality and visual effects, and has always made continuous efforts and breakthroughs in technology development, visual creation, production processes, and visual effects company management processes.

Reputation and quality are the core values that Maodong Studio adheres to, and in the past few years, Maodong Culture's works have been highly recognized by all parties.
